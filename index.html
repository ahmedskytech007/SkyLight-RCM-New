The Real Problem

The Compliance Tightrope

Medical coding sits at the intersection of clinical documentation, payer policy, and compliance regulation. Under-coding leaves legitimate revenue on the table every single day. Over-coding creates audit liability that can result in federal recoupment demands, civil monetary penalties, and exclusion from Medicare and Medicaid programs. The ICD-10, CPT, and HCPCS code sets are updated annually, specialty-specific coding guidelines are complex and constantly evolving, and payers layer their own coverage policies on top of published code descriptors. Staying current, accurate, and compliant requires dedicated expertise that most practices simply cannot sustain in-house. SkyLight provides that expertise — at scale, for every encounter, for every specialty.
